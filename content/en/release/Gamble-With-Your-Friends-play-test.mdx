## Movement and Survival: Why Crossing the Street Wins or Loses Runs

In many sessions, movement risk is just as important as bet selection. Losing cash or time during travel can erase a profitable cycle.

### Safe transit framework

| Step | Execution | Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| **Scout lanes first** | Pause, read traffic rhythm | Reduces random impacts |
| **Commit in bursts** | Sprint through clean windows | Faster than hesitation |
| **Avoid greed routes** | Don’t detour for minor purchases | Preserves timer |

### Timer discipline checklist

- If &lt;4 minutes remain, stop experimenting.
- If carrying enough to clear debt, route to ATM immediately.
- If you die and respawn, switch to recovery mode (safe cards, no high-variance side games).
- If heart-rate is unstable, normalize before any high-risk travel segment.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Most “unlucky” losses are actually route management failures. Movement + timing discipline often beats aggressive gambling.
