## Managing Your Bankroll and Quota

The ultimate goal in most sessions is to meet your quota before the time runs out. The pressure to perform often leads players to make reckless decisions, such as going "all in" on unfavorable odds. To succeed, you must coordinate with your team. If one player is on a losing streak, they should focus on low-stakes games to prevent further damage to the group's collective wallet.

### Strategic Group Play
*   **Designate a "Banker":** One player should hold the majority of the funds to prevent accidental over-spending.
*   **Communication is Key:** Always announce your intention to use an item before the bet is placed.
*   **The "Leave" Threshold:** Establish a rule where the group retreats once a certain profit margin is reached, regardless of how "lucky" you feel.
