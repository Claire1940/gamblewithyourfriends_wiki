## Gamble With Your Friends guide: Core Team Roles That Prevent Throws

Most failed runs aren’t caused by bad luck—they’re caused by everyone doing everything at once. Assign roles before day start.

| Role | Main Job | Secondary Job | Common Mistake |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Shot Caller** | Decides when to leave after quota | Calls “stop betting” windows | Chasing bonus goals too late |
| **Banker** | Tracks team net gain/loss | Caps max bet size by phase | Silent while teammates spiral |
| **Item Captain** | Buys/rerolls ticket items | Announces activation timing | Burning actives without team setup |
| **Enforcer** | Holds bat/gun for intervention | Pulls griefers off machines | Using force too early |
| **Flex Gambler** | Plays objective-friendly games | Swaps to high EV when buffed | Ego all-ins after one win |

### Fast pre-run checklist
1. Set a **daily max individual loss** (example: 1,000).
2. Decide who controls ticket purchases.
3. Decide who can trigger high-impact items.
4. Agree on “quota reached = freeze bets unless called.”

> **Warning:** Your lobby’s biggest enemy is momentum tilt. Once one player starts chain-betting, others mirror it. Interrupt that behavior quickly.

## Social Control: How to Stop One Player From Nuking the Run

Co-op casino games are people management. Build soft and hard controls.

### Soft controls
- Public bankroll callouts every 60–90 seconds.
- “Red light” command: nobody presses any machine.
- Positive reinforcement for discipline (not just big wins).

### Hard controls
- Temporary removal from machine zones.
- Enforcer role with bat/gun when a throw is in progress.
- Forced regroup in elevator/limo when quota is secured.

| Situation | Best Response | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| Player keeps saying “one more spin” | Immediate red light + move squad | Breaks momentum loop |
| Shop chaos and bad buys | Freeze purchases for one day | Protects ticket EV |
| Quota met but team keeps gambling | Shot caller initiates exit countdown | Clear authority prevents debate |
| Team is broke | Stop all high variance games | Prevents total wipeout |
