## Best Early-Game Strategy for Consistent Profit

Most teams in **Gamble With Your Friends gameplay** bleed money in the first two days from confusion, not bad luck. Set rules before entering the limo.

### 1) Assign fixed roles
- **Caller:** decides when to stop betting and when to exit  
- **Primary bettor:** executes planned wagers  
- **Safety holder:** carries rewind/insurance/protect-loss tools  
- **Disruptor:** can physically block tilted teammate bets if needed

### 2) Use a bankroll ladder

| Team Bankroll State | Bet Size Plan | Objective |
|---|---|---|

### 3) Set a “tilt lock”
If one teammate loses multiple max bets in a row, they rotate out for one minute. This single rule dramatically improves **Gamble With Your Friends gameplay** outcomes in co-op groups.

> **💡 Tip:** In messy public-style sessions, the best “advanced tactic” is actually enforcing stop-loss and table rotation.
