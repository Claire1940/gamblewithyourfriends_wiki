## The First Hurdle: Start Menu Achievements

Most players miss their first trophy before the game even loads. When you boot up the game, you are greeted with a prompt asking, "Are you feeling lucky?" This isn't just flavor text; it is a mechanical choice that dictates your first accomplishment.

| Action | Result | Achievement Progress |
|---|---|---|
| **Select "Yes"** | Flips a coin. If it lands on No, the game closes. | Progresses "True Gambler" |
| **Select "No"** | The game starts normally. | Unlocks **"Honest Mistake"** |

Hitting "No" is the most reliable way to enter the lobby and secures an easy achievement for being honest about your lack of luck. However, persistent players who repeatedly try to "win" the coin flip by selecting "Yes" can unlock a separate hidden milestone for successfully entering the game against the odds.

## Body Part Removal and Ticket Farming

In *Gamble With Your Friends*, tickets are the currency used for cosmetic upgrades like the Propeller Hat, the Super Saiyan Mustache, or the Henchman Suit. However, if your group runs out of money and fails to meet the daily quota, you may need to resort to the "Surgery Station" to earn tickets through sacrifice.

### Achievement: "The Cost of Failure"
This achievement is unlocked when a player sells their first body part to cover a debt or earn tickets.

1.  **Eyes:** Selling an eye renders half of the player's screen black.
2.  **Mouth:** Removes the ability to use in-game proximity chat (though Discord users can bypass the social penalty).
3.  **Legs:** Forces the player to ragdoll or "roll" around the lobby, making navigation significantly more difficult.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Restoring body parts costs a significant amount of tickets. Only sacrifice parts if you are desperate to buy the "Gun" or "Coin" items for the next round.

## Casino Game Milestones

The core of the **gamble with your friends achievements** list revolves around your performance in the various casino games. Each game has specific win conditions that trigger trophies.

| Game | Achievement Name | Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| **Duck Racing** | **Yarl's Salvation** | Win a race betting on the underdog duck, Yarl. |
| **Blackjack** | **Under the Radar** | Win a hand with a value of less than 15. |
| **Slots** | **Triple Threat** | Hit a jackpot on three different machines in one day. |
| **Plinko** | **Gravity's Rainbow** | Hit the maximum multiplier (24x) on the Plinko board. |
| **Craps** | **Hot Hands** | Roll a 7 or 11 on the first roll three times in a row. |

### Strategy for Yarl's Salvation
Yarl is statistically the weakest duck in the racing mini-game. To unlock this achievement, players often have to "sacrifice" several rounds of betting minimum amounts on Yarl until the RNG favors him. It is recommended to do this on Floor 1 where the stakes are lower and the quota is easier to manage.

## Lobby Secrets and Hidden Trophies

The lobby is more than just a place to wait for friends. It is a multi-floor environment filled with Easter eggs and platforming challenges.

### Finding Bing Bong
One of the most sought-after **gamble with your friends achievements** involves finding the secret NPC known as Bing Bong (or Bang Bang). 
*   **Location:** The roof of the main casino building.
*   **How to reach:** Players must use the trampoline in the backyard or perform a series of diagonal jumps across the balcony railings.
*   **Reward:** Interacting with Bing Bong triggers a unique voice line and the "Legally Excited" trophy.

### The Mirror Dimension
If you customize your character to look exactly like another player in your lobby (matching colors and hats), you can unlock the "Identity Crisis" achievement. This is easiest to do with the default "orange" or "pink" colors found in the bathroom tubs.

## Advanced Item Synergy

To reach the final floors where the quota exceeds 5 billion dollars, you must use items effectively. Using these items correctly also unlocks high-level achievements.

*   **The Gun:** This item allows you to "shoot" the quota or NPCs. Using the gun to skip a quota requirement when you are in the red unlocks "Armed Robbery."
*   **The Devil's Reel:** This item triples your last win but carries a risk of losing everything. Successfully tripling a win of over 1 million dollars unlocks "Deal with the Devil."
*   **The All-In Coin:** Provides a free "all-in" bet that doesn't consume your actual balance if you lose. Using this on a losing bet to save the run unlocks "Never Punished."

| Item Combo | Strategy | Result |
|---|---|---|
| **Gun + Quota** | Use when balance is < 10% of goal. | Forced progression to next floor. |
| **Revert Loss + Crash** | Use in the Rocket/Crash game. | Safety net for high-multiplier bets. |
| **Microphone + Slots** | Sing nearby to increase luck/payouts. | Achievement: **"Vegas Performer"** |

## Reaching the Final Floor: The Boss Encounter

The ultimate **gamble with your friends achievements** are found on Floor 5. Once you meet the final massive quota, you enter a standoff with the "Boss of Gambling."

> **💡 Tip:** On the final floor, the ATM is your only source of income. You must manually carry sacks of money to the "Pay Your Debt" or "Double or Nothing" zones.

To unlock the "True King of Games" achievement, your group must choose the "Double or Nothing" path and win a final coin toss. If you lose, the game resets your progress entirely, making this the highest-stakes achievement in the game.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I unlock gamble with your friends achievements in single-player?**

A: While most games can be played solo, several achievements like "Identity Crisis" or "Dab Me Up" require at least one other player in the lobby to trigger.

**Q: What happens if I lose my mouth and can't talk?**

A: In-game, your voice will be muffled or silenced, but this does not affect your ability to earn achievements. You can restore your mouth at the surgery station for approximately 20 tickets.

**Q: Is there a way to guarantee a win in Duck Racing for the Yarl achievement?**

A: No, the races are entirely RNG-based. However, using the "Stink Ray" item on opposing players' favored ducks can slightly increase the odds of a chaotic upset.

**Q: How do I access the higher floors?**

A: Higher floors are unlocked by surviving consecutive days. Floor 2 typically opens on Day 4, and Floor 3 on Day 7, provided you have met the daily money quota.
