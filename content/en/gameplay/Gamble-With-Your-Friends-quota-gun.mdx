## Understanding the Quota Gun Mechanic

The fundamental loop of the game revolves around reaching a specific financial target, known as the "Quota," within a set number of gambling rounds. If a player or a team fails to reach this threshold, the **Gamble With Your Friends quota gun** is introduced during the "Settlement Phase." 

Unlike standard RNG mechanics, the quota gun is influenced by how far a player is from their goal. The closer you are to the quota, the lower the "chamber density"—essentially the number of live rounds loaded into the device. If you are significantly under the quota, the odds of a "misfire" or a "safe click" diminish rapidly.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I completely avoid the Gamble With Your Friends quota gun?**

A: Yes. If you meet or exceed your credit quota for the round, the gun is never drawn. The goal of the game is to stay "In the Green" so the Settlement Phase is skipped entirely.

**Q: Does the number of players affect the gun's lethality?**

A: Indirectly, yes. In larger groups, the total quota is higher, which means individual failures are more common. However, the gun itself always follows the chamber density rules based on the percentage of the quota met.

**Q: What happens if everyone fails the quota?**

A: If the entire lobby fails, the game enters "Sudden Death" mode. The **Gamble With Your Friends quota gun** is passed around in a circle until only one player remains or the collective debt is paid through "Blood Credits."

**Q: Are there any secret codes to jam the gun?**
